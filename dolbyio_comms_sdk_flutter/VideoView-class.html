VideoView

VideoView class Null safety Widgets

A widget that can display a MediaStream for a Participant.

You can use VideoView in two ways, either as an item of a GridView or a ListView used with the VideoView.withMediaStream constructor or as a stand-alone widget outside of collection widgets, such as GridView or ListView. In this second option, you need to use the VideoView constructor and provide a VideoViewController to the constructor.
